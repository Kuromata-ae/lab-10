Doner. What is this?

A Middle Eastern dish of meat (chicken, lamb, mutton, less often pork), grilled on a spit, wrapped in lavash or pita, with cabbage, pickled/pickled cucumber, onion, garlic sauce and, optionally, spices. It is consumed without the use of cutlery.
